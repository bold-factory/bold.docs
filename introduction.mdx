Bold Factory is a cloud-based industrial platform built specifically for manufacturing SMEs. It unifies Production Control (MES), Planning & Purchasing (MRP), Warehouse Management (WMS), Maintenance (CMMS), AI Agents, and Analytics into a single system — so you can leave behind the spreadsheets, the paper trails, and the disconnected tools that slow your factory down.

## What Bold Factory does

Bold Factory is designed to take you from a factory running on paper and Excel to a fully digitalized operation — without a lengthy or expensive implementation project. Here is the journey most customers follow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Book a demo and choose your plan">
    Schedule a demo with the Bold Factory team to see the platform in action. Once you're ready, pick the plan that fits your factory size — **Lite** (€300/mo) or **Pro** (€500/mo) — or talk to sales for an Enterprise arrangement. There is no lock-in and setup is free.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Onboard with your dedicated hours">
    Every plan includes dedicated onboarding hours (15 h on Lite, 25 h on Pro). The Bold Factory team works with you to configure your processes, import your data, and train your team so you are operational as quickly as possible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live and keep improving">
    Once live, your team manages production, planning, warehouse, and maintenance from any device, anywhere. AI Agents automate routine tasks in the background, while Analytics gives you the visibility to continuously improve your operations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Bold Factory is built for operators as much as managers. Its mobile-first interface means your shop-floor team can start using it on day one — no lengthy training programmes required.
</Tip>
