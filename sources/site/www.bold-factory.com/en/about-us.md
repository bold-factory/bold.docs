## About us

Get to know the story behind Bold Factory and our mission to transform the manufacturing industry.

![About Bold Factory](http://www.bold-factory.com/_next/image?url=%2F_next%2Fstatic%2Fmedia%2Fabout-us.d03732d0.png&w=3840&q=75)

Bold was born from a reality shared by thousands of manufacturing SMEs: a lack of visibility into their processes, dependence on paper and spreadsheets, and the struggle to find tools that truly adapt to their pace and way of working.

### We are more than operations management software.

We are a platform that brings order, agility and clarity to your production, planning and inventory. But above all, we are a partner for bold leaders who want to make their mark on the industry — not just hit KPIs.

### We believe in a more human, connected industry where simplicity drives change.

We believe the future of industry is not about complexity, but about intelligent simplicity. That's why we built a modern, intuitive and deeply human solution — designed so that every factory, regardless of size, can be more efficient, more connected and more free.

Be brave, be Bold.
