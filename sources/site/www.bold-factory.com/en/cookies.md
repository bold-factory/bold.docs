## What is a cookie?

Cookies are small text files that websites transfer to your browser and store on your device. These tools allow the website to retain and retrieve data about your visit, including language preferences and other settings, in order to optimize the services offered and improve your user experience while browsing.

## Use of cookies on the website

Our website uses both first-party and third-party cookies to improve the quality of the services we offer by analyzing users' browsing patterns. The use of cookies is subject to the personal settings you choose, determining which types of cookies you wish to enable. In this regard, your consent constitutes the legal basis for the use of cookies during your browsing.

It is important to note that, in order to enjoy an optimal browsing experience, it is necessary to keep cookies enabled. If you prefer not to receive cookies, you can select the “Reject all” option in the banner that appears on screen.

## Which cookies are used?

BOLD implements various types of cookies: first-party, where we are responsible for processing, and third-party, where responsibility lies with external entities.

First-party cookies are managed directly by BOLD and are subject to the limitations specified in our [privacy policy](http://www.bold-factory.com/en/privacy 'privacy policy'), both with regard to data processing and to the user's rights over the information contained in the cookies.

Third-party cookies are managed by external entities, and the transfer of data contained in these cookies to third parties is governed by the guidelines established in our [privacy policy](http://www.bold-factory.com/en/privacy 'privacy policy').

Following the recommendations of the Spanish Data Protection Agency, below we detail the specific use of cookies on our platform, providing the most complete information possible.

### Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for the basic operation of the website, enabling essential functions such as navigation between pages and access to different sections. Without these cookies, the website cannot operate properly.

Strictly necessary cookies do not process personal information and are therefore not subject to data protection regulations.

- **cc\_cookie**: Stores the choice to accept or reject the use of other cookies

### Statistical analysis cookies

Statistical cookies allow website owners to better understand how visitors interact with their pages, collecting and providing information anonymously.

- **\_ga**: Registers a unique Google Analytics ID in order to generate statistical data about how the visitor uses the website.
- **\_gcl\_au**: Stores Google Tag Manager data about conversions made through the website for statistical purposes.
- **\_fbp**: Meta (Facebook) pixel identifier to measure visits and advertising campaigns.
- **\_fbc**: Meta pixel identifier when the user accesses from a Facebook link.

## How can I block or delete the cookies used by this website?

In addition to using our customizable settings panel, you can manage, block or delete the cookies installed on your device through your web browser settings.

If you do not want your data to be collected by Google Analytics, you can install an opt-out add-on for your browser. Find more information [here](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/181881?hl=en&ref_topic=2919631 'here').

Disabling analytics cookies does not prevent the user from effectively using the website.

To make configuration easier, we provide links to the main browsers with detailed information on managing cookies:

- [Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/61416 'Google Chrome')
- [Mozilla Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences 'Mozilla Firefox')
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/en-us/guide/safari/sfri11471/mac 'Safari')
- [Microsoft Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/delete-and-manage-cookies-168dab11-0753-043d-7c16-ede5947fc64d 'Microsoft Edge')

We remind you that these links may be updated or modified by the browser developers.

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the correct operation of the website or limit access to certain functionalities.
