## Dashboards

Charts, tables, filters… you can do everything a modern Business Intelligence tool should offer.

You can not only build dashboards and KPIs, but also drill into your data to understand what's happening on your shop floor — all from anywhere in the world, on any device.

## Custom Reports

Bold comes with a set of reports we consider essential for any company — but what if you need more?

No problem. You can build your own reports with the powerful editor included in the tool, with no extra licences or additional costs.

Best of all, you won't need to learn a new way to model data — Bold uses PowerBI to generate reports and dashboards.
