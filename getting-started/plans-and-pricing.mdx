## Understanding AI Credits

AI Credits are the currency that powers Bold Factory's AI Agents. Every time an AI Agent performs an automated action on your behalf — generating a production schedule suggestion, flagging a purchasing anomaly, drafting a maintenance report, or running a demand forecast — it consumes a small number of credits.

Your plan includes a monthly credit allowance that resets at the start of each billing cycle. Credits do not roll over from month to month.

| Action type | Approximate credit cost |
|---|---|
| Demand forecast run | ~50–200 credits |
| Purchase order suggestion | ~20–50 credits |
| Production schedule optimisation | ~100–500 credits |
| Maintenance report generation | ~30–100 credits |

<Note>
  Exact credit consumption depends on the complexity of the task and the volume of data processed. Your AI Agents dashboard shows a real-time breakdown of credit usage so you can monitor consumption at any time.
</Note>

### AI Credit top-up packs

If you run low on credits before the end of the month, you can activate an additional credit pack. Packs are billed monthly and can be added or removed at any time — there is no commitment.

| Pack | Credits per month | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Standard top-up | 5,000 credits/mo | €100/mo |
| Large top-up | 20,000 credits/mo | €250/mo |

<Tip>
  If you consistently need top-up packs every month, upgrading your plan is likely more cost-effective. Moving from Lite to Pro, for example, gives you 15,000 extra credits per month plus significantly higher usage limits — for just €200/mo more.
</Tip>

## Upgrading between plans

You can upgrade your plan at any time from within your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately — you gain access to the higher limits and credit allowance from the moment the upgrade is confirmed, and your billing is prorated for the remainder of the current month.

To upgrade:

1. Go to **Settings → Subscription** in your Bold Factory account.
2. Select your new plan and review the updated pricing.
3. Confirm the upgrade. Your new limits apply instantly.

<Note>
  Downgrading from Pro to Lite is also possible, but takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Make sure your usage (users, SKUs, locations) is within Lite limits before downgrading to avoid disruption.
</Note>

## Enterprise plan and custom pricing

The Enterprise plan is designed for larger manufacturing businesses or groups that need unlimited scale, custom integrations, dedicated account management, or bespoke onboarding programmes. Pricing is tailored to your specific requirements.

To discuss Enterprise, [contact the Bold Factory sales team](https://bold-factory.com/en/contact). A specialist will reach out to understand your needs and prepare a custom proposal.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I try Bold Factory before committing to a subscription?">
    Yes. Bold Factory offers a guided demo so you can see the platform in action with your own use cases before signing up. [Book a demo](https://bold-factory.com) to get started — there is no obligation to subscribe after the demo.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="If I start on the Lite plan, can I upgrade to Pro later?">
    Absolutely. You can upgrade from Lite to Pro at any time from within your account settings. The upgrade takes effect immediately and your billing is adjusted on a prorated basis. All of your existing data, configurations, and users carry over automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a lock-in period or minimum contract?">
    No. Bold Factory is billed monthly with no lock-in. You can cancel or change your plan at any time without penalties. Your subscription continues until the end of the current billing month.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods are available?">
    Bold Factory accepts major credit and debit cards. Enterprise customers can arrange invoice-based payment. Contact the sales team if you have specific payment requirements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does the price include integration with my existing ERP or systems?">
    All plans include access to Bold Factory's integration capabilities. Standard integrations are covered within your onboarding hours. Complex or bespoke integrations may require additional scoping — your onboarding specialist will let you know during the kickoff call if this applies to your setup.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will I get access to new features automatically?">
    Yes. Bold Factory is a continuously evolving cloud platform. New features and improvements are deployed regularly and become available to all subscribers automatically — there are no upgrade fees or version migrations to manage.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?">
    Your data remains accessible for 30 days after your subscription ends, giving you time to export what you need. After 30 days, your data is permanently deleted from Bold Factory's servers in accordance with the data processing agreement. If you need a specific data export format, contact the support team before cancelling.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
